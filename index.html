Recent Work

Current research covers the intersection of geoeconomics and institutions. At the broadest level, it relates the origins of institutions in US economic history to their function today. At the narrowest, it includes modelling the effects of market-facing policy like minimum wage. On the side, there are tools and ideas for pedagogy.
